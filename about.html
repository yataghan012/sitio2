WHO I AM

Tomás P

Born in Córdoba, Argentina, in 1998. After graduating from high school, he began working in orthopedics as a neuronavigation technician in operating rooms. Later, he went freelance, working in electrical and electronic security, where he was responsible for marketing and sales. He acquired technical knowledge to improve his online presence and boost sales. In 2026, he started working as a freelance web designer. Specialized in providing one-stop services that include photography, design, and coding. Passionate about helping businesses grow and succeed online.

02

PASSION

  • DESIGN

    Great products and services can feel like pure magic — transforming complexity into simplicity, solving real problems in ways that seem almost effortless. But even the most powerful magic needs a wand. That's how I see design: not as decoration, but as the essential bridge between a brilliant solution and the people who need it. Every day, I strive to craft interfaces that feel intuitive, invisible, and alive — experiences that put that magic directly into the user's hands, shaped perfectly to fit them.

  • TECHNOLOGY

    As the saying goes, "Any sufficiently advanced technology is indistinguishable from magic." And I believe that wholeheartedly. Technology has an unparalleled power to reshape the world and transform lives — and that potential drives everything I do. I'm committed to staying ahead of the curve, embracing change not as a challenge, but as an opportunity. Because innovation doesn't spread on its own: it's the shared mission of engineers and designers alike to bring it to the people who need it most. That's the spark I bring to every project.

  • STORY

    Behind every great product lives a narrative — a journey from confusion to clarity, from frustration to delight. Design and brand are not just visual choices; they are the chapters, the rhythm, and the voice that guide a user through that journey. I believe that the most powerful interfaces don't just function — they resonate. They make the user feel understood, seen, and accompanied. When design and story become one, a product stops being a tool and starts being an experience worth remembering.
